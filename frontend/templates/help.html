Help-Page




1.How to Sign In:

First you need to click on the upper left at "Sign Up"
->then you need to fill all fields with your data
->then click on the button "Submit"
=>Now you should have successfully signed up


2.How to Log In:

First you need to click on the upper left at "Log In"
->then fill in your email and your password
->then if you are sure your Data is correct, click on the button "Login"
=>now you should have been loged in and be redirected to the Home Page


3.How to use the Booking Site:

First you need to click on the upper left at "Booking"
->the green buttons with a chair, are the seats that are available to get booked
--->to book a seat you want, click on the button and commit that you want to book it
->the red buttons with a chair, are your booked seats
--->to decline your reservation you can click on this button and you need to confirm it
->the "X" are the seats that got booked by other users
